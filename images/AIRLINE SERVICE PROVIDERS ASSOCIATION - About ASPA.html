ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO

The Association Is A Nonprofit Mutual Benefit Corporation For The Purpose To
  • Represent the common business interests of the corporation’s members
  • Advocate for better relations among the members and government agencies and authorities having jurisdiction over United States commercial airports
  • Balance the use and regulation of resources to ensure economic prosperity and environmental protection of the country
  • Foster the highest degree of safety, training, operational efficiency, business conditions and cooperation among the members and government agencies and authorities
  • Encourage the interchange of ideas, educate and inform the community-at-large concerning issues of concern to the members
  • Provide a forum for the discussion, examination and resolution of issues of concern to the members​
